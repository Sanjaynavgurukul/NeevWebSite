Neev

NeeV, a learning centre for the under-served, is a programme run under Bharat Memorial Charitable Trust (BMCT)

BMCT Trust

BMCT was founded by Dr. Kesho Ram, an industrialist, and his wife Ms Santosh Gupta in the memory of their late son Bharat

Mission

To motivate and support as many under-served as we can, for education, good health, vocational training & employment

Vision

We strive to provide the same in a happy, caring and stimulating environment to help them identify their place under the sun
